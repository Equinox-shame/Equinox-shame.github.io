英语观点选择作文模板

七月 01, 2022

题目：毕业后找工作 or 创业

第一段

With the rapid development/ globalization of（——填：society, culture, medical service, education, economy）

引出问题，背景：Nowadays（youngsters/students）have different options upon （——例子：graduation.）

例举选择：Some think that（——例子：to find a job somewhere）is beneficial, while others think（——例子：to start a business of their own）is a better choice.

做出选择：Personally, I prefer the former/ latter.

The following reasons can account for my preference.（过渡句）

第二段

论点1：First and foremost, there is no doubt that（——例子：to find a job somewhere is more free.）

论据1：Based on big data, a majority of（successful social elites have）admitted that （——例子：their careers are free.）

论点2：Furthermore/moreover/additionally, it is obvious that（——例子：we can make useful friends when we have a decent体面 job.）

论据2：Where there is/are（——例子：is a good job）**, there is/are** （——例子：are friends）.

改编自“有志者事竟成”：Where there is a wil, there is a way.

论点3：Last but not least, no one can deny that（——例子：to find a job is easier than to start a business.）

论据3：Although/though （——例子：some people do succeed, most entrepreneurs/bosses failed when they start.）

虽然…但是…

第三段

重申观点：In a word, A is better/more suitable than B .

提出希望：If we spare no efforts to（不遗余力）（——例子：work hard in our career）the future of （our life） will be promising and rosy.
